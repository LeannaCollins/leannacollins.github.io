About

Welcome to the world of mushrooms. Here we will discuss one of my favorite topics.

Mushrooms can be found just about anywhere you look. If it is grassy, wet, dark, and littered with decomposing organic matter, you are sure to find a variaty. But which mushrooms are okay to forge out in the wild? I will tell you which to look for and the characteristics of many types.

Fun Fact: Did you know that the largest mushroom in the world is the underground network of a Honey Mushroom (Armillaria ostoyae)? It is located in Oregon's Malheur National Forest and spans over several square miles, estimating to weigh thousands of tons. It is even considered the largest living organism on Earth. How cool is that!
